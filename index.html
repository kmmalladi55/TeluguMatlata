ℹ️ How to Play the Game

📜 Registration – Why Register?

  • 1️⃣ Track your score and progress, especially when participating in competitions.
  • 2️⃣ Receive a Telugu Word of the Day via email to start your day with fresh learning! 🌅
  • 3️⃣ Help us spread the love for Telugu by sharing with friends and family who cherish the language.
  • 4️⃣ Your feedback matters! Share your thoughts to make the game even more fun and engaging. 🎉

⏩ Skip Registration – Just Play & Enjoy!

Not ready to register? No worries! Click **Skip Registration** and jump straight into the game. Have fun exploring Telugu words! 🎮

📂 Game Categories

We’ve carefully crafted four exciting categories for you to explore. 🌟

  • 🔹 We’ll keep expanding and adding more words over time.
  • 🔹 Planning a special event or social gathering? Let us know, and we’ll add words related to your occasion! 🎊

🏆 Score & Game Rules – Play, Learn & Win!

🎯 Your Goal → Guess the word by revealing each letter in 4 attempts.

✅ Scoring System:

  • 🔵 1st Attempt → 100 points
  • 🟢 2nd Attempt → 75 points
  • 🟡 3rd Attempt → 50 points
  • 🟠 4th Attempt → 25 points
  • 💡 Missed all 4 attempts? Don’t worry! The system will reveal the letter and still award you 25 points.

📢 Game Progression:

  • ✅ Keep revealing letters until the full word is displayed.
  • ✅ No additional points for completing the word.
  • ✅ No negative points! This game is about celebrating Telugu—so no penalties! 🥳

👀 Smart Hint:

Clicked a letter and it’s not greyed out? Try clicking it again—it might earn you 100 points! 😉

🗂 Category Selection:

  • 🔹 Click any category to start or continue playing.
  • 🔹 To keep the fun and excitement going, rotate the categories!
  • 🔹 Once all words in a category are completed, it greys out, and you’ll be prompted to choose another category.

🚪 Ending the Game:

  • ✅ Click **End Game** whenever you’re ready to stop.
  • ✅ Close the window/tab once you’re done.
