🎮 How to Play Telugu Word Game

This is exclusively for kids who started learning Telugu at their tender age.

  1. Click on Kids Click Here to start.
  2. Select any category of your choice.
  3. You will see a clue in English.
  4. Two rows appear with boxes – the bottom row has shuffled Telugu letters.
  5. Click the correct letters in order. If you go wrong twice, the system helps you!
  6. Finish a word and watch 🎉 confetti! You can...
    • Continue words in the same category
    • Or choose a different one by clicking Pick Different Category
  7. Click End Game to stop and close the game anytime.
